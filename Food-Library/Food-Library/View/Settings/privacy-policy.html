Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 25.12.2024

At Food-Library ("we," "our," or "us"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us when using our mobile application Food-Library (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of your information as outlined in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information directly from you when using the App. The only data we collect is through the use of third-party services, such as Firebase, for performance and functionality improvements.

1.1 Firebase Analytics and Services

We use Firebase, a third-party service provided by Google, to collect and analyze certain non-personal information. Firebase may collect the following types of data:

  • Device Information: Firebase may collect information about the device you use to access the App, such as the device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: Firebase collects anonymous data on how you interact with the App, such as usage patterns, feature interactions, screen views, and the duration of App sessions.
  • Crash and Error Data: Firebase Crashlytics collects data on crashes or errors within the App to help us improve its performance and fix bugs.

1.2 No Collection of Personal Information

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII), such as your name, email address, or any other information that could identify you, unless you voluntarily provide it (e.g., through a support request or feedback form). However, we strongly recommend that you avoid sharing any personal information via the App unless absolutely necessary.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the data collected by Firebase for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality and Performance: We use data to monitor and enhance the performance and stability of the App.
  • Usage Analysis: Firebase analytics helps us understand how the App is being used, which features are most popular, and how we can improve the user experience.
  • Bug Fixes and App Improvements: Crash data helps us identify and resolve errors or crashes, improving the App’s overall stability.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. The information collected by Firebase is used solely for the purposes described above.

We may share data with third-party service providers like Firebase, but only in accordance with their privacy policies. Firebase’s use of data is governed by their own privacy policies, which you can read here.

We may also disclose your information if required by law, such as in response to a court order or governmental request.
